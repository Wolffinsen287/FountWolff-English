Meet your teacher!

FWE's Founder and Head Instructor, Daniel Wolff, began his career as a New York City legal proofreader, and has since turned his expertise in the English language into an opportunity to help professionals and enthusiasts just like you in their linguistic endeavors.

With a solid educational background in pedagogy, Wolff's unique approaches to teaching and learning focus on individualized instruction, giving each student creative and, at times, unconventional ways to reach previously-unattainable goals in their quest for English proficiency.

Our Method

At FountWolff English, we believe that language learning should be as unique as you are. Our innovative approach combines proven pedagogical techniques with personalized instruction to help you achieve your English language goals.

1

Individualized Assessment

We begin by understanding your current level, learning style, and specific goals. This allows us to create a customized learning path tailored to your needs.

2

Real-World Application

Our lessons focus on practical, real-world scenarios relevant to your professional and personal life. You'll practice English in contexts that matter to you.

3

Creative & Unconventional

We use innovative teaching methods that go beyond traditional textbooks, making learning engaging and memorable through interactive activities and practical exercises.

4

Continuous Feedback

Regular assessments and constructive feedback help you track your progress and identify areas for improvement, ensuring steady advancement toward fluency.
